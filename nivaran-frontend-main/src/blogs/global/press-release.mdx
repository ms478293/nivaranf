## Introduction to Nivaran Foundation:

Nivaran Foundation is a non-governmental organization with an office in CTC Mall, Sundhara, Kathmandu, Nepal. Registered as a non-profit organization with the company registrar, the Nivaran Foundation is a global campaign focusing on ending poverty and inequality. The foundation envisions achieving prosperity and equality through community engagement, networking, public-private partnerships, and multi-stakeholder coordination. With a focus on community empowerment, NF employs a holistic approach to create lasting, positive change.
