## Discover How You Can Make a Lasting Impact

The Nivaran Foundation is on a mission to create sustainable and transformative change in Nepal, tackling critical issues in healthcare, education, and child welfare. But they can’t do it alone. Your support—whether it’s financial, time, or energy—helps them make real, lasting improvements in the lives of communities across Nepal. Your involvement truly matters, and there are many ways you can contribute. Take a look at the different ways you can join this life-changing movement and be part of something extraordinary.

    <p>

      contributions, you’ll be a vital part of ensuring that families and
      children in Nepal receive the continuous care and opportunities they

    <p>

      little bit counts and helps make a big difference.
    </p>
    <h2>Fundraise for Nivaran Foundation</h2>
    <p>
      Want to do something special? Create your own fundraising campaign!

      others to get involved. Fundraising is a fun and impactful way to spread
      the word about the Nivaran Foundation's mission and generate much-needed
      resources for their work.
    </p>
    <h2>&nbsp;Corporate Sponsorship&nbsp;</h2>
    <p>

      Nivaran Foundation through corporate sponsorships or grants. By
      contributing to specific projects, your company will not only help fund
      important initiatives but also demonstrate a strong commitment to
      corporate social responsibility. Your support can go a long way in
      creating meaningful change for communities in Nepal.
    </p>
    <h2>&nbsp;Volunteer Your Time and Skills</h2>
    <p>
      Sometimes, the most valuable thing you can give is your time. Whether
      you’re helping organize events, assisting with social media, or working
      directly with the foundation’s programs, your time and skills are an
      essential part of their success. Volunteering is an incredibly rewarding
      way to make a tangible impact, no matter where you are.
    </p>
    <h2>&nbsp;Leave a Legacy Through Planned Giving</h2>
    <p>
      Imagine leaving a lasting impact on future generations. Planned giving or
      bequests allow you to continue supporting the Nivaran Foundation long
